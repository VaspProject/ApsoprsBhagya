Registration Guidelines

APSOPRS Registration Fee includes:

  • Admission to all scientific sessions
  • Admission to the exhibition
  • Admission to Lunch during the event
  • Daily Coffee Breaks
  • Certificate of Attendance
  • Gala Dinner

Payment Method

  • Payment by credit card is available only through the on-line registration system.
  • The following credit cards will be accepted Visa, Master card, American Express.
  • All fees are quoted in USD for international delegates
