Visa Information

IMPORTANT : Please check your expiry date on your passport. It is necessary to have an eligible passport with a minimum validity of 6 months from the intended arrival date. From Visa-required Countries.

Invitation Letter

We would like to inform you that the APSOPRS 2026 secretariat is pleased to provide an official invitation letter to those who need it for Visa application to visit India. After completing registration, in-person participants may request a letter by sending a mail to the conference secretariat apsoprs2026@gmail.com. Please direct any questions to your nearest Indian Embassy or Consulate regarding entry to India, as the terms and conditions may vary.

Visa Letter Application Terms

Please note that the Letter is not a commitment on the part of the organizer to provide any financial support. The official invitation letter, to be used for application of visa, will be issued ONLY to those who have completed the registration payment.

📝 Documents Required

  1. Passport – Valid for at least 6 months from the date of arrival, with 2 blank pages.
  2. Visa Application Form – fill online at : https://indianvisaonline.gov.in
  3. Conference invitation letter – From the Indian host APSOPRS 2026
  4. Proof of residence – In your country (utility bill, ID, etc.)
  5. Passport-sized photo – As per Indian visa photo specs
  6. Travel itinerary – Flight tickets or tentative plans
