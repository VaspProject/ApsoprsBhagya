Registration Guidelines

APSOPRS Registration Fee includes:

  • Admission to all scientific sessions
  • Admission to the exhibition
  • Admission to Lunch during the event
  • Daily Coffee Breaks
  • Certificate of Attendance
  • Gala Dinner

Payment Method

  • Payment by credit card is available only through the on-line registration system.
  • The following credit cards will be accepted Visa, Master card, American Express.
  • All fees are quoted in USD for international delegates

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • If applicants need to change the information or cancel the registration, it must be submitted in writing via email to the Secretariat : apsoprs2026@gmail.com
  • All refunds will be processed in 30 days after the completion of the conference according to the following:
  • After 23:59 (IST), Oct 18th 2026 registration cancellation request will not be accepted
