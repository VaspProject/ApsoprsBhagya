Dr. Kasturi Bhattacharjee

PRESIDENT APSOPRS

Dear Esteemed Colleagues,

It is with profound joy and heartfelt pride that I welcome you to the APSOPRS 2026 Congress, to be held in Assam—a land where timeless traditions and modern progress flourish amid the tranquil beauty of nature.
As President of this prestigious Society and Organising Secretary of the meeting, I take immense pride in inviting you to a vibrant academic platform where we unite to explore, exchange, and elevate the science and art of oculoplasty. Our theme, "VistaQuest in Oculoplasty – Reconstruct, Redefine, and Revolutionize,"reflects our shared commitment to innovation in reconstructive and aesthetic periocular care.
The congress will feature immersive hands-on workshops, expert-led symposia, thought-provoking case deliberations, and the unveiling of cutting-edge technologies—each meticulously curated to foster dynamic learning and enhance clinical excellence.
We are privileged to host distinguished international speakers from the United States, Europe, and across Asia, whose contributions will broaden our perspectives and stimulate meaningful global collaborations.
While the scientific program promises to be intellectually stimulating, we warmly invite you to experience the unique spirit of Assam—its rich cultural heritage, warm hospitality, and natural charm.
We look forward to welcoming you to an academically enriching and culturally vibrant APSOPRS 2026 Conference at the Mayfair Spring Valley Resort, Guwahati, Assam, India, from 19th–21st November 2026.

Warm regards,
Dr. Kasturi Bhattacharjee
President, The Asia Pacific Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery
(APSOPRS)

Explore the Vibrant Northeast Experience

❮ ❯ ❰ ❱ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯ ❯

Guwahati, the gateway to Northeast India, is a city that seamlessly blends natural beauty, rich culture, and exciting tourism opportunities. Located on the banks of the Brahmaputra River, Guwahati offers breathtaking views, vibrant markets, and a glimpse into Assam's rich heritage.
Experience the enchanting allure of natural beauty along the Brahmaputra River, where you can indulge in a serene river cruise or leisurely wander its scenic banks. The majestic Kamakhya Temple, perched atop Nilachal Hill, offers breathtaking panoramic views that captivate the soul. Additionally, the picturesque Deepor Beel, a stunning wetland and bird sanctuary, invites nature lovers to immerse themselves in its tranquil surroundings, making it a perfect escape for those seeking peace and beauty.
Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Assam, where the culinary landscape is a feast for the senses. Delight in local specialties such as masor tenga, a tangy fish dish, and pitha, a traditional rice cake that embodies the region's culinary heritage. The vibrancy of Assamese life is further showcased through its traditional festivals, including the exuberant Bihu and the grand Durga Puja, each offering a unique glimpse into the local customs and communal spirit. A visit to the revered Kamakhya Temple, a significant Shakti pilgrimage site, adds a spiritual dimension to your experience, inviting reflection and reverence amidst its sacred ambiance.
Experience the rich cultural tapestry of Assam by visiting the revered Kamakhya Temple, a significant pilgrimage site known for its spiritual ambiance. Explore the Assam State Museum, where the region's history and heritage come alive through captivating exhibits. The Umananda Temple, perched on an island in the Brahmaputra River, offers a serene escape and stunning views. For nature enthusiasts, the Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary presents an opportunity to witness the majestic one-horned rhinoceros in its natural habitat. After immersing yourself in these cultural and natural wonders, indulge in a shopping spree at Fancy Bazaar, Pan Bazaar, and the vibrant local markets, where you can find exquisite handicrafts and unique souvenirs that reflect the essence of Assam.
Whether you're interested in history, culture, nature, or cuisine, Guwahati has something for everyone. Come and experience the warmth and hospitality of Assam !
