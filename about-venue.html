About Mayfair Spring Valley Resort

Nestled in the picturesque valley of Tepesia, Sonapur, Mayfair Spring Valley Resort is a serene escape from Guwahati's hustle and bustle. Surrounded by verdant hills and lush greenery, this resort offers a tranquil retreat for couples, families, and business travelers alike.

Amenities at Mayfair Spring Valley Resort

SPA

The state-of-the-art Mayfair Spa offers an exceptional spa and salon experience, complete with modern amenities and expert therapists. Indulge in rejuvenating treatments, massages, and therapies to unwind and relax.

Swimming Pool

Take a refreshing dip in the pool, perfect for unwinding after a long day. The pool area is surrounded by lush greenery, providing a serene ambiance.

Gym

Stay fit and active with the resort's well-equipped gym facilities, featuring modern equipment and trainers.

Restaurant and Bar

Savor unique dining experiences at the BayLeaf Restaurant and Baron & Baroness Bar, situated amidst lush greenery with breathtaking views. Enjoy local and international cuisines, along with an extensive selection of beverages.

Conference and Banquet Facilities

Host corporate gatherings, weddings, and social events with ease, thanks to the resort's top-notch facilities. The resort's event team will ensure that every detail is taken care of.

Lounge

Relax and unwind in the resort's comfortable lounge, perfect for reading, chatting, or simply enjoying a cup of coffee.

24-hour Room Service

Enjoy convenient room service throughout your stay, with a range of options to suit your taste and preferences.

Luxurious Accommodations

The resort offers a range of luxurious rooms, each designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay. From spacious rooms with garden views to lavish villas with private pools, every accommodation is equipped with modern amenities and luxurious touches. Each room is meticulously designed to provide a luxurious experience, with attention to detail and a focus on comfort and relaxation. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, Mayfair Spring Valley Resort's luxurious rooms are the perfect retreat.
