Guidelines for Abstract Submission

We are inviting our colleagues to submit abstracts for oral (YAPSOPRS), e-poster, and video presentations for the APSOPRS 2026 Meeting in Guwahati, Assam, India.

  • All abstracts must be uploaded on the Online Submission System by April 30th (Thursday), 2026.
  • E-mail submissions will not be accepted.
  • For any inquiries, please contact the APSOPRS 2026 secretariat via e-mail apsoprshelpdesk@gmail.com
    OR Call Us : +91 422 4374920 || +91 422 4372920.

Language

  • Abstracts must be written and presented in English.
  • Registration is mandatory to submit abstract.

Title

  • Use a concise title that indicates the content of the abstract.
  • Titles should start with a capital letter and should not exceed 100 characters (including spaces).
  • Refrain from using abbreviations in the title.

Authors

  • Name and surname for each author must be provided. (David Sharma / Incorrect: D. Sharma)
  • Do not include degrees or titles.
  • The first author will be the presenting author. The first (presenting) author should register and attend the meeting.
  • The first author is responsible for securing consent from patients whose clinical data will be included in the final presentation.

Affiliations

Each author should be listed by institution, and country.

Content

  • Abstract content for free paper and E posters.
  • Abstract Content (excluding title, authors, and institutions) should include the following headlines upto 1000 characters (including spaces.) in each of the following sub headings. PURPOSE, METHODS, RESULTS, and CONCLUSIONS.
  • Do not include graphs, tables, images, or references in the abstract.

Others

  • One can submit maximum of 2 abstract in each category of oral paper presentation and in video presentation as a first author. However there is no limit on how many abstracts can be submitted by a single author on e poster.
  • A confirmation e-mail will be sent to the presenting author with Abstract ID and Password on receipt of your submission. If the presenting author does not receive the confirmation email within 24 hours, please contact apsoprshelpdesk@gmail.com .
  • The abstract cannot be edited after submission.
  • APSOPRS 2026 local organizing committee holds the copywrite to all materials presented at the Meeting.

YAPSOPRS (Young APSOPRS member) Criteria

Presenters must meet one of the following:

  • An APSOPRS member who is 8 years or less out of fellowship training
  • An APSOPRS member who is 40 years of age or younger
  • An APSOPRS fellowship-in-training in member countries

Please Note: An APSOPRS member by category C becomes a temporary member of APSOPRS and is required to be an APSOPRS member after fellowship training.

Ethical Considerations :

Mention if your research involves human subjects and confirm adherence to ethical standards.

Time Allotted For Free Paper :

Free paper - 6 MINUTES +2 MINUTES of discussion

Video Presentations :

  • For video submission one needs to upload the video in the link of abstract submission before 30th April, 2026.
  • The duration of the video should be maximum of 4 mins.
